HEARTS2

In late december 2017, we played some more hearts. This time we only made it to 24 games, but collected good data nonetheless. After studying how to design and build visualizations, I wanted to build something that'd look better and do more (and be more semantically correct) than bar and line charts plucked from the Highcharts library. I threw this together using SVG and some spaghetti javascript. This time I won, not to brag, which almost makes up for how badly I lost last time.
